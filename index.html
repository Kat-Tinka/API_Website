Nutrition Facts and Health Benefits:
Broccoli is very low in calories, providing only 31 calories per cup (91 grams). The nutrition facts for 1 cup (91 grams) of raw broccoli are ( 3 ):
  • Calories: 31
  • Water: 89%
  • Protein: 2.5 grams
  • Carbs: 6 grams
  • Sugar: 1.5 grams
  • Fiber: 2.4 grams
  • Fat: 0.4 grams
